# Research Core Config Reference

Environment variables are loaded by `research_core.config.ResearchCoreConfig`.

## General
- `RESEARCH_CORE_ENABLED`
- `RESEARCH_CORE_STORAGE_ENABLED`

## Replay
- `REPLAY_ENABLED`
- `REPLAY_STEP_GRANULARITY` (`tick|second|bar_close`)
- `REPLAY_ALLOW_DEGRADED_INPUTS`
- `REPLAY_MAX_DIVERGENCE_WARNINGS`

## Simulation
- `SIMULATION_ENABLED`
- `SIMULATION_PARALLEL_SCENARIOS`
- `SIMULATION_MAX_VARIANTS`
- `SIMULATION_INCLUDE_TRANSACTION_COSTS`
- `SIMULATION_INCLUDE_SLIPPAGE_MODEL`
- `SIMULATION_INCLUDE_PARTIAL_FILLS`

## Calibration
- `CALIBRATION_ENABLED`
- `CALIBRATION_METHOD` (`isotonic|platt|binned`)
- `CALIBRATION_MIN_SAMPLE_SIZE`
- `CALIBRATION_REFRESH_TRADES`
- `CALIBRATION_SCOPE_STRATEGY`
- `CALIBRATION_SCOPE_INSTRUMENT`
- `CALIBRATION_SCOPE_SESSION`
- `CALIBRATION_SCOPE_REGIME`
- `CALIBRATION_SCOPE_SETUP_TYPE`
- `CALIBRATION_FALLBACK_GLOBAL`
- `CALIBRATION_NUM_BINS`

## Meta Approval
- `META_APPROVAL_ENABLED`
- `META_APPROVAL_MIN_SCORE`
- `META_APPROVAL_MIN_CALIBRATED_WIN_PROB`
- `META_APPROVAL_MIN_EXPECTANCY_PROXY`
- `META_APPROVAL_REJECT_IF_UNCALIBRATED`
- `META_APPROVAL_ALLOW_DELAY_MODE`
- `META_APPROVAL_DEFAULT_DELAY_SECONDS`
- `META_APPROVAL_DOWNSIZE_MULTIPLIER`
- `META_APPROVAL_HARD_REJECT_ON_EVENT_CONFLICT`
- `META_APPROVAL_HARD_REJECT_ON_EXECUTION_DISLOCATION`

## Reports
- `REPORTS_ENABLED`
- `REPORTS_OUTPUT_DIR`
